THE UNTOLD STORY OF IPAJA

The name (ipaja) was coined out distinctly from the description of the new settlement erected among the woods of the thick forest area called (IGI-OPA-AJA) meaning a very strong and straight tree used as roofing rafters on walls before roofing.

Since the demand for rafters (IGI-OPA-AJA) was very high as people came from Lagos lsland all the way via Badagry, the name gave way to (Abule lgi-opa -aja) meaning the village for rafter trees.

The customary nature of Yorubas to shorten names gave rise to unconscious removal of letters "GIO" from (IGI OPA AJA) and (IPAJA) became the name of the village.

Chief Oderinde became the first HIGH CHIEF of Ipaja between 1840-1900 while Chief Akiniyi Labelabe became the second HIGH CHIEF of Ipaja between 1900 -1931.

THE BIRTH OF ISHAGA AKINIYI KINGDOM

During reign as the HIGH CHIEF of lpaja kingdom, specifically in the year 1926, HIGH CHIEF Akiniyi Labelabe located and established ISHAGA AKINIYI KINGDOM. The kingdom is located in the present day (Ado-Odo otta local government) area of Ogun state Nigeria, It share boundary wIth liogboto and Adewolu thlichment of the kingdom, the land was town, Other neighboring towns are (Orogan) and Agric.

At the early years majorly used as a farmland for cocoa and other cash crops. HIGH CHIEF Akiniyi oversees the activities of the kingdom from his palace in lpaja royal kingdom. After the demise of his royal highness the HIGH CHIEF of lpaja kingdom, HIGH CHIEF Akiniyi Labelabe, the overseeing and management of Ishaga Akiniyi kingdom was between the sons of HIGH CHIEF Akiniyi from 1931.

The following were the names and times of the managers of the kingdom.

  • JOHNSON OGUNDINA AKINIYI 1931-1980
  • JAMES FADIPE AKINIYI 1980-1993
  • KAREEM ADEBAYO OPEIFA 1993-2006
  • OBA SYLVESTER AJIBOLA AKINIYI (ONIPAJA OF IPAJA KINGDOM) 2006-2014

Note that the first daughter of HICH CHIEF Akiniyi Labelabe was Madam Morinsola Akiniyi who gave birth to baba Kareem Adebayo Opeifa who manages the kingdom from 1993-2006.

After the demise of Oba Sylvester Ajibola Akiniyi, the Oba of Ipaja, the kingdom of lshaga Akiniyi was left under general management by the family until November 30th, 2019. This is the day for the coronation of the first HIGH CHIEF of lshaga Akiniyi kingdom by Onilogbo kingdom, Oba Oluwafemi Ojugbele.
