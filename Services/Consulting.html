FEMI ROYALS CONSULTING

Femi Royals Consulting is set up to consult for Embassies and High Commissions on setting up of Bilateral/Multilateral Chambers of Commerce/Business Council The Consultancy also set up Foreign Trade Academy.

Femi Royals Consulting is in partnership with some Foreign countries to set up a Foreign Languages village in ISHAGA AKINIYI KINGDOM The countries are, France( French language), Brazil and Portugal (Portuguese language), China(Chinese language) Germany (German language), Italy (Italian language) and Spain (Spanish language)

The aim is to introduce our youths to different foreign languages aside from English language so to open different opportunities to them technologicaly and business development wise a lot of opportunities would be tapped through this channel Femi Royals Consulting shall expose many businesses minded individuals/Organised Private Sectors to opportunities globally through Trade Fair in different parts of the world, Trade missions/visit, Seminars, workshop and convention outside Nigeria . This shall be done through Print and Electronic media. We are also running a Welding Certification centre.

CORPORATE OBJECTIVES OF FEMI ROYALS CONSULTING

  1. Drawing up of Strategies, programmes and policies regarding the commercial policy, studying the trends in foreign Market from the point of view of commercial connectivity and opportunities.
  2. Supporting the presence of Nigerian economic operators in international Market.
  3. Cooperation with NACCIMA, MAN, chambers of commerce & Industry, Professional Associations in the field of foreign trade, work among institutions responsible for investment promotion.
  4. To ensure the promotion of Nigerian interest in relation with other counties and economic regional organizations in the field of international commerce.
  5. To gather support for the presences of Nigerian economic operators in international Market.
  6. To work assiduously for the promotion and support of exports and elaboration of the National strategy for exports in public - private partnership, in compliance with the NEPC directives and world trade organization agreements.
  7. To render services related to the registration and an authorization of companies and activities, information on the business legislation in Nigeria, consultancy and assistance on the formalities for companies, Associations or foundations registration and authorization of the activities in collaboration with the appropriate institutions/agencies.
