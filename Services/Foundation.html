MISSION OF THE FOUNDATION

Femi Royals Foundation is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as a non-government organization (NGO). The foundation is located within the Ishaga Akiniyi community. Under the auspices of the foundation.

Four major centers shall be built as focus. The four centers are as follows:

  1. CULTURAL CENTRE
  2. YOUTH DEVELOPMENT CENTRE
  3. FOREIGN LANGUAGE CENTRE
  4. ISHAGA AKINIYI CHAMBERS OF COMMERCE INDUSTRY AND AGRICULTURE

    1. THE CULTURAL CENTRE

      2. The cultural center is meant to develop the culture of the people of Ishaga Akiniyi kingdom. It also meant to showcase the kingdom in order to attract visitors alongside other objectives such as Creating jobs and develop skills of the locals. Attracting and retaining business Revitalizing the community and Developing talents. In showcasing the kingdom, efforts shall be made towards the development of the traditional heritage of the people in areas such as the history, traditional meals and festivals of the kingdom. For every visitor to the kingdom, there will be good time visiting the local traditional food vendors who always delight in serving delicious local meals such as pounded yam using cocoyam with egunsi olokisi and bush meat to complement an undiluted palm wine! We understand that by taking in culture in any form whether it be music or art, we gain better understanding of humanity as a whole and of the groups we live amongst. As a result of this, culture can help break down boundaries, this is the focus of the cultural center! At the Ishaga Akiniyi cultural center there is no boundary as all are welcome to enjoy the rich culture of the kingdom.

    2. THE YOUTH DEVELOPMENT/EMPOWERMENT CENTRE

      3. The youth development and empowerment center is basically sub divided into

      • Skills acquisition center.
      • Sports and relaxation Arena.

      A. Skills Aquisition Center

      At the skills acquisition center the focus shall be establishing youth development and empowerment programs aimed at creating healthier and higher qualities of ife for underprivileged or at-risk youths. It is a general understanding that lack of skills is a major cause of corruption in any society hence the decision to have skills acquisition in five major sectors at the Femi Royal Foundation's acquisition center. The importance of this endeavor is to enable the beneficiaries be self-employed, have diverse job opportunities, generate employment and collectively reduce crime in the society.

      The five sector are:

      1. Welding and Auto mechanic.
      2. Fashion management.
      3. tailoring and Cosmetology.
      4. Barbing and Hair dressing.
      5. Catering & hotel management.

      B. Sports and Relaxation Arena

      The presence of sport arenas and stadiums play a significant role in defining the image of a kingdom like Ishaga Akiniyi Royal Kingdom. We understand that the presence of sport and relaxation arenas can act as a catalyst for the renewal of close neighborhoods. The arena will in ways help take the youths off crimes and the streets. The youths can engage their energy in sports and thereby discover their hidden talents. Sport arena's will also stimulate an economic growth and renewal in major ways like direct spending, multiplier benefits and indirect effects. At the kingdom four major sport arena shall be the focus namely:

      • Football Pitch.
      • Lawn Tennis court.
      • Basketbal/ volley ball court.
      • Traditional sport.

    3. FOREIGN LANGUAGE CENTRE

      4. Language, culture and economy are quite inseparable because of their intimate relationship. In the process of learning a foreign language one learns their culture and tradition. A good knowledge of any foreign language may serve as catalyst for economic empowerment and development for the youths.

      At The Femi Royals Foundation, partnership is being formed with the prestigious in state who develop and oversees Obafemi Awolowo University (0AU) lle-Ife, Osun the curriculum and materia for studies in the agreed foreign languages. It's been arranged that every student who successfully finished courses in any of the foreign language at the center can have a direct entry into OAU to complete a degree in such language

      The four foreign languages for consideration are:

      1. French Language
      2. Chinese Language
      3. German Language
      4. Portuguese Language

      1. French Language

        2. French is along with English language the only language spoken on all five continents. More than 220 million people speak French on all the five continents. It's a major language of international communication. It the second most widely learned language after English and the sixth most widely spoken language in the world. French is also the second most widely taught language after English and is taught on every continent. France is considered often as the language of culture. Studying and learning French language opens up opportunities for higher education at some of France's best universities often on favorable financial terms. In partnership with the France embassy in Lagos and The Franco-Nigerian chambers of commerce alongside Aliance Francaise , the Foundation is poised to train youths in French language to open a world of opportunities to them.

      2. Chinese Language

        3. Learning Chinese language becomes more and more important with the growth of Chinese economy. There is about one fifth of the world populalation in China. The chinese are dominating globally in different segments like global business, industry and technology. Therefore training and learning Chinese language increases the economic worth of individuals. To expand its global influence, China is investing heavily in developing nations around the world. The rise of China has brought about the necessity for more Chinese language speakers across the globe and especially in Africa where China is making serious inroads.

        In partnership with the Chinese embassy and China's language teaching giant Guoji Dui , the foundation is embarking on massive training and teaching of youths in Chinese language.

        Best students will be selected at the end of the course and awarded study grants and sponsorship for cultural trips to China to enable them hone their language skills and immerse themselves in the unique Chinese culture.

      3. German Language

        4. Knowledge of German language increases job opportunities with German and foreign companies within and outside the country. Germany is the second most commonly used scientific language. Germany is the third largest contributor to research and development. Learning German provides the learner with an insight into the way of life, the hopes and dreams of people in German speaking countries. Learning German language open opportunity to study and work in Germany as Germany awards a generous number of scholarship and other support to study in Germany. Femi Royals Foundation is partnering with the German embassy, Nigeria-German council and the Goethe institute Nigeria to train and empower youths for a successful and benefitting training and learning of German language for a productive life and future.

      4. Portuguese Language

        5. Portuguese is one of the most useful language in the world. It is actually spoken by 5% of the world population. Portuguese is so much complete that every Portuguese speaker is able to understand and speak Spanish very easily. Portuguese is the sixth most spoken language worldwide, with 215 million native speakers-mostly in Portugal and Brazil also several African countries. The embassy is ready and wiling to empower and help as many youths take advantage of learning the Portuguese language fora productive life. Several exchange program are being arrange after the completion of the training period.
