THE OBJECTIVE OF THE CHAMBER

The major objective of the chamber is to bring corporate business to the grassroots. It is to form a business network whose goal is to further the interest of the local community businesses and to advocate on behalf of the business community. The function of the chamber is to promote interest in local business possibilities of the Ishaga Akiniyi community. The chamber shall work for industrial and business development which support residents in order to improve the economic, civic and cultural well-being of the kingdom. The chamber shall periodically arrange business events, conferences and expo with the aim of developing and empowering the people.
