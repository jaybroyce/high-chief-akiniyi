About the High Chief of Ishaga Kingdom

Prince Femi Akiniyi of Ishaga Kingdom

High Chief FEMI FRANCIS AKINIYI was Born to the royal family of  Pa Simeon Alabi Akiniyi and Alhaja Hawanat Aduke Akiniyi (NEE Abbas Abina Iposu of Epe land, Lagos) on the 17th December, 1976. Prince Femi Francis Akiniyi became the first High Chief of Ishaga Akiniyi kingdom on the 30th November, 2019.

BRIEF HISTORY OF THE FIRST HIGH CHIEF OF ISHAGA AKINIYI

He was born a prince and a bonafide heir to the prestigious throne of Ipaja Royal kingdom. He had an adventurous and robust childhood experience beginning his life at Ipaja, living with his paternal grandmother, Madam Dorcas Akiniyi (A.KA mama Alakara)

He began his educational journey at St Judges UNA school Ipaja from 1976 - 1981, He moved to the then Awori Anglican Comprehensive High School, lpaja now called Anglican Comprehensive High School from 1981 to 1986.

After a successful secondary school education, Prince Femi Akiniyi moved to Federal School of Arts & Science in Victoria Island, Lagos in 1988 for a year programme in Remedial courses. In 1989 he gained admission to the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU), lle-lfe in Osun State Nigeria for a Degree course in English Arts which he finished in 1995. He had his mandatory National Youth Service between 1996 and 1997.

HIS WORK LIFE

After his mandatory Youth Service, he went to Abeokuta in Ogun State for a teaching job in 1988. Just after his experience teaching in Abeokuta, he got a part-time lecturing job with lbadan polytechnic's distant learning center from 2001-2003 and also started his learning center where he teaches and train students for o'level examinations. He had a brief time working at Ayoola Concord in Abule-Egba, Lagos. He later established Femi Royal Global Concept Limited, a company duly registered with the coporate affairs commission on the 21 July, 2006. The company majors in Trade & Investment, Oil & Gas and general business brokerage.

He became the first crowned High Chief of lshaga Akiniyi kingdom on the 30th of November, 2019. Since the inception of his reign, it had been his desire to place the kingdom on world map and make the kingdom a destination for both local and international visitors, hence the vision to establish the Femi Royal Foundation.
